Core Features

Everything You Need

A complete attendance management solution built for modern Ethiopian schools.

Student Management

Add, edit and manage students with class, section, grade and contact information. Max 30 students per section.

Mark Attendance

Mark Present, Absent or Late for each student. Filter by class, section, month and year. QR code scanning support.

Reports & Analytics

Daily, monthly and comparative reports. Subject-wise attendance breakdown with visual progress bars.

Role-Based Access

Admin, Teacher and Student roles with separate dashboards and permissions. Students can only view their own data.

Smart Notifications

Automatic low attendance warnings. Students get notified when their attendance drops below 75%.

QR Code Scanning

Generate QR sessions for instant attendance marking. Students scan once — attendance recorded automatically.

About SAMS

SAMS (School Attendance Management System) is a modern web-based platform designed to simplify attendance tracking for schools. Built with Django, it provides a secure, role-based system for administrators, teachers and students.

  • Built with Django & Bootstrap 5
  • Dark & Light theme support
  • Ethiopian phone number validation
  • Class capacity enforcement (max 30)
  • QR code attendance scanning
